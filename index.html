Welcome to Node Dominance!


How to play:

1. Node Dominance is a two-player game.

2. Each player will take turns drawing a line between dots and then adding a new dot on the path.

3. Each dot may only have 3 branches.

4. You may NOT cross any line while joining dots.

5. The last player to make a legal move WINS!!!

Try to isolate nodes to block your opponent!

Feel free to adjust the rules to match your playstyle!
